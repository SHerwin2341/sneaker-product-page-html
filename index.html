About the Aero Low

The Aero Low started as a side project. Our lead designer had one question: what's the lightest shoe we can build without it feeling cheap? Two years and fourteen prototypes later, this is the answer. The knit upper is woven from a single piece of recycled polyester yarn — no overlays, no glued-on panels, nothing unnecessary.

The gum sole is vulcanised natural rubber sourced from a family-run plantation in Malaysia. It ages beautifully, grips better as it wears in, and develops a slightly amber tint over time that's unique to how you walk. No two pairs end up looking exactly the same after six months of wear.

How to Find Your Size

The Aero Low fits true to size for most people. If you are between sizes or have a wider foot, we recommend sizing up half a size. Follow these steps to measure your foot at home before ordering.

Measure your foot in 4 steps

  1. Place a blank piece of paper flat on the floor against a wall. Stand on it with your heel touching the wall.
  2. Have someone mark the tip of your longest toe with a pencil, or press a pen down yourself while standing.
  3. Measure the distance in centimetres from the wall edge of the paper to your pencil mark. This is your foot length.
  4. Match your measurement to our size guide: 25cm = UK 7, 25.5cm = UK 7.5, 26cm = UK 8, 26.5cm = UK 8.5, 27cm = UK 9, 27.5cm = UK 9.5, 28cm = UK 10.

Still unsure? Contact our sizing team or visit our full size guide.
